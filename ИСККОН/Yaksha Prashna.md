Yaksha Prashna – Answers That Transcend Time By Govinda Das

1. Who makes Sun to rise? - Brahma. Brahma means Vedas. Vedas make Sun to rise (Sun also means knowledge)
2. Who are the associates of Sun? - Devatas - the controlling principle (Sun also indicates awakening of Atma Tattva)
3. Who makes Sun to set? - Dharma makes Sun to set. Consistently the Sun practices dharma and sets.
4. How does Sun stabilize himself in space? - Satya (truth) makes him stable.
5. How does on become "shrotriya"? - Veda adhyayana (study, chanting, and recitation of the Vedas) makes one shrotriya
6. What makes one achieve fame? - Tapasya makes one famous and mahatma
7. How does one take help from others? - Dhairya or Courage makes one receive help. Help comes by looking for such support, calling out. Seek you shall find.
8. How does one become buddhiman (wise)? - By serving Vriddha (aged) one becomes wise.
9. What is devatva (divinity) for a Brahmana? - Adhyayana (Study of Vedas) is devatva for a Brahmana
10. What is dharma for sat-purusha (Pure Being)? - Tapasya is his sat-dharma (Eternal Way)
11. What is their limitations or human-ness? - Death is their limitations
12. What makes them asat? - Accusations are his asat (adharma)
13. What is devatva (divinity) for a Kshatriya? - Study of Sastra vidya (battle art) is his devatva
14. What is their sat dharma? - Yajna is his sat-dharma
15. What is their limitations or human-ness? - Fear is their limitations
16. What makes them asat? - Foresaking those seek shelter is his asat dharma
17. What are the resources for Yajnas (sacred fire)? - Prana is the sama
18. What is the main Yajasva for Yajna? - Mind is the yajasva
19. Who carries yajna? - Rk is the carrier of yajna
20. What is that even Yajna cannot transcend? - Yajna cannot go beyond Rk
21. What is important for Farming? - Rains
22. What is important for those who sow? - Seeds
23. Those who want pura (town) to grow, what do they need? - Cows
24. Those who have potential to give birth, what is highest for them? - Being mother
25. Who is considered lifeless even after having sensuality, good intelligence and wealth? - One who doesn't offer havisva to Devatas, Guests, Pitris, bhratyas, Oneself. They are living dead.
26. What is heavier than Bhumi? - Mother
27. What is taller than sky? - Father
28. What is faster than wind? - Mind
Что превосходит скорость ветра? - ум (мысль)
29. What is more in number than grass? - thoughts
30. Which living being doesn't close its eyes even though sleeping? - Fish
31. Even though born who doesn't move? - Egg
32. Who doesn't have heart? - Stone
33. Only through movement it grows? - River
34. Who is a friend of a traveler? - fellow traveler
35. Who is a friend at home? - Wife
36. Who is a friend for diseased? - Doctor (Vaidya)
37. Who is friend for dying? - daiva
38. Who is the guest of all beings? - Agni is the Athithi
39. What is Sanatana Dharma (eternal duty)? - The path that shows mukti is Sanatana Dharma
40. What is nectar? - The milk coming from Soma Havisya  (cow) is nectar
41. What constitutes this universe? - Entire creation is Vayu (air)
42. Who travels alone? - Sun
43. Who is born again and again? - Moon
44. What is the medicine for snow/cold? - Fire
45. What is the container that holds everithing? - Bhumi
46. Where does Brahma reside? - Expertise and Rujutva
47. How to get success? - Charity gives fame and success
48. What is the road for svarga? - Satya (truth) is the bridge to go to svarga
49. Where is sukha/happiness? - Sheel (Good behavior)
50. Who is the atma (soul) of the man? - Putra (son) is atma
51. Whom did God ordain man as a friend? - Wife
52. Which is main sadhan (resource) for life? - Water filled clouds
53. How does a man achieve his destination? - Charity is most important path
54. Which is the greatest sadhana for wealth earning? - courage/saamarthya
55. Which is the greatest wealth? - Greatest wealth is knowledge of scriptures
Каковым является главное из всех богатств? - Знание! Ведь его не украдет вор, и оно лишь возрастает, когда им делишься.
56. Which is the best profit? - Health is the greatest profit
57. Which is the best sukha (happiness)? - Satisfaction
58. What is that by giving up one becomes dear to others? - By giving up ego one becomes dear to all
От чего нужно отказаться чтобы люди нас любили? - От гордости.
59. What is that by giving up is freed from pain and misery? - One who controls anger does not become miserable
60. What is that by giving up one become wealthy? - By giving up desires one becomes wealthy
От чего нужно отказаться чтобы разбогатеть? - От желаний.
61. What is that by giving up one become sukhi? - By giving up greed and false hope one becomes sukhi
От чего избавившись человек может обрести счастье? - Жадность
62. Why does one give charity to brahmana? - For Practicing dharma
63. Why one give charity to actors and dansers? - To give them facility to grow in fame success
64. Why one gives charity to servant? - For their sustainability
65. Why one gives charity to king? - Avoiding the wrath one gives taxes (For obtaining relief from fear)
66. What is covering of the entire universe? - Universe is covered ignorance (Darkness)
67. What is it that prevents from lighting it up? - Because ignorance there is no light
68. What separates friends? - Greed separates one from friends
69. Through which some does not attain heaven? - Too much of attachment separates one from svarga
70. What is death for man? - Poverty is dead
71. What is death for Rasthra (country)? - Bad governance is the death of country
72. How does shardha (puja) becomes dead? - Shardha is dead when stotriyas are not there
73. When yagna is called as dead? - Yagna without daksina is dead
74. What is the direction? - Saints are direction
75. What does one call as water? - Sky is water
76. What is food? - Cow is the root of all foods. Cow gives milk, which produce butter ghee, which is used in Yajna, sacrifices. The sacrifices are causes of clouds, which bring rain. Rain is essential for producing food from seeds, (corps).
77. What is poison? - Desire are poison
78. What is right time for shraddha (puja)? - Inviting one who has studied Veda Adhyayana will make one to do the shraddha any time.
79. Can you explain the characteristics of Tapasya? - Svadharma practice is Tapasya
80. What is dama or atma-sayyama? - Controlling mind is dama
81. Which is the greatest of the Ksama (forbearance)? - Tolerating dvandva or dualities is great
82. What is the fruit of shyness? - Not doing what not ought to be done is lazza
83. What is called as jnana? - Tattvarta understanding is Jnana
84. What is shama (somposure and continence; mental equilibrium; peacefulness)? - Equanimity is shama
85. What is daya (mercy)? - Sarvabhutahita (Wishing happiness for all living creatures) is daya
Что означает милосердие? - Проявлять заботу о благе все живых существ.
86. What is arjavam (simplicity or transparency)? - Chitta which is equipoised is arjavam
87. What is the unconquerable enemy? - Anger
88. What is a disease that has no end? - Lobha (greed) is endless disease
Какая болезнь неизлечима? - Жадность
89. Who is sadhu? - Sadhu is one who is sarvabhutahita (Wishes well for all creatures)
Кого можно считать честным? - Тот, кто желает блага всем живым существам.
90. Who is asadhu? - Hard heartedness caused by not having Karuna (compassion)
Кого можно считать нечестным? - Корыстного человека.

93. What is laziness? - Dharma inaction is laziness

95. What is called as sthairya (steadiness) by rishis? - Not shaking for svadharma is Sthairya
96. What is called as dhairya (patience)? - Controlling senses is patience (dhairya)
Что такое терпение? - Терпение — это способность обуздывать свои чувства
97. What is best of snana? - Washing mental contamination is best snana
98. What is called as dana? - Jiva Raksha (Protecting all creatures) is best charity
99. Who is pandit? - One who knows dharma is a Pandit
100. Who is called as an atheist (nastik)? - A fool is an atheist (nastika)
101. Who is a fool? - An atheist (nastika) is a fool
102. What is kama (desire)? - That which is the cause of samsara chakra
103. What is asuya (jealous)? - Heart burning is called Asuya
104. What is ahankara? - Ignorance is ahankara
105. What is damba (pride)? - Posing oneself as a great dharmatma (Virtuous Person) is damba
106. What we call a parama daiva and what is it? - Dana is called parama daiva
107. What is paishuna (wickedness)? - Accusing and finding faults in others is paishuna
Что такое порок? - Порок - это злословие. Человек проецирует то что у него в уме на других. И когда мы видим изъяны в других, это значит что эти недостатки есть у нас самих.
108. How do you integrate: dharma artha, kama - which are opposite to each other? - Just like apparently dharma and wife are opposite to each other but are harmonized by husband. Similarly 3 vargas can be integrated
109. Who is the object of eternal hell? - One who invites brahmana for bhiksha and regrets is subjected to eternal hell. One who has falsity against: Vedas, Dharma Sastras, Brahmana, Devata, and Pitra dharma. Out of greed and lack of satisfaction one who rejects brahmanas says "I have nothing to give" experience hell
110. How does one acquire brahmanatva? Is it by Kula, activities, self studying, or hearing of sastras? - Not by Kula, not by studying, not be hearing alone, but by proper conduct one becomes brahmana. Therefore brahmana should focus on sadacara. Once whose sadacara is lost, his brahmanatva is lost. One who reads one who teachers and those who simply discuss are fools but one who practices sastra is a pundit. One who has studied 4 Vedas but bad behavior he is lower than low. But one who is Nitya Agnihotri and self controlled is real brahmana.
Что делает человека брахманом: происхождение, ученость или образование? - Ничего из этого, только поведение делает человека брахманом.
111. What do the sweet taker receive? - They become dear to all
112. What does one get one who has "vishvasa" (one who does with proper thinking)? - They become mostly successful
113. What does one get having many friends? - They are happy and satisfied
114. One who is dharma nistha what does he get? - Dharma Nistha achieves sat-gati
115. Who is happy? - One who doesn't have debt, one who does not stay in foreign land, even though eats once in few days in his own house, is said to be happy
116. What is surprise? - Everyday jivas leave this world, but those who stay, strive to stay forever there is nothing greater surprise than this
Что является самым удивительным в мире? - Каждый день живые существа умирают. Никому не избежать этой участи, но те, кто остаются в живых, все-таки думают, что сами они никогда не умрут. 
117. What is path? - The path of the maha-bhagavata is the true path for all.
118. What is news? - The tidings are that in this world–which is like a cauldron with the sun as its fire, days and nights as its fuel, and months and seasons as its wooden ladle–all creatures are being cooked by time.
